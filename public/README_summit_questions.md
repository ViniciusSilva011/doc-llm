# Luxury Design & Craftsmanship Summit — Question and Answer Dataset

## Event Overview

### 1. What is the Luxury Design & Craftsmanship Summit?

The Luxury Design & Craftsmanship Summit is an event organized by Covet Group that brings together creative minds, designers, makers, and brands from around the world to discuss the future of luxury design and craftsmanship.

### 2. When did the Luxury Design & Craftsmanship Summit take place?

The summit took place from 20 to 21 June 2018.

### 3. Where was the Luxury Design & Craftsmanship Summit held?

The event was held at Pavilhão Multiusos de Gondomar, near Oporto, Portugal.

### 4. Who organized the Luxury Design & Craftsmanship Summit?

The summit was organized by Covet Group.

### 5. What was the purpose of the summit?

The purpose of the summit was to present new perspectives and innovative practices in luxury and design, while highlighting craftsmanship as a key factor in the growth and elevation of the luxury design industry.

### 6. What kind of experience did the summit offer attendees?

The summit offered attendees the opportunity to listen to testimonies from inspiring designers and successful entrepreneurs, allowing them to apply that knowledge in their personal or professional lives.

## Nicole Segundo and the Michelangelo Foundation

### 7. Who represented the Michelangelo Foundation at the summit?

Nicole Segundo represented the Michelangelo Foundation at the summit.

### 8. What is the Michelangelo Foundation’s mission?

The Michelangelo Foundation was created to revitalize fine craftsmanship, launch a cultural movement around its values, and connect the worlds of design and craftsmanship to support the future economic viability of craftsmanship.

### 9. What is Nicole Segundo’s educational background?

Nicole Segundo graduated from the London School of Economics with an MSc in Media and Communication and has a BA in European Studies with French and Italian from Bath University.

### 10. What professional experience does Nicole Segundo have?

Nicole Segundo built her experience in craftsmanship through the high-end Swiss watchmaking industry, working for brands such as Graubel Forsey, Girard-Perregaux, Parmigiani Fleurier, Corum, and Hublot in senior management, communication, sales, and marketing roles.

### 11. Why is Nicole Segundo’s international experience relevant?

Nicole Segundo lived in several countries across four continents, giving her broad life experience that enriches her perspective on luxury design and craftsmanship.

## Conceição Amaral and FRESS

### 12. Who represented FRESS at the summit?

Conceição Amaral represented FRESS, the Fundação Ricardo Espírito Santo.

### 13. What is Conceição Amaral’s role at FRESS?

Conceição Amaral has been director of FRESS since 2006 and became Chairman of the Board of Directors in 2015.

### 14. When was FRESS created and by whom?

FRESS was created in 1953 by Ricardo Espírito Santo Silva, an art collector who donated part of his private collection to the Portuguese state.

### 15. What was the original purpose of FRESS?

FRESS was created as a school museum to spread and preserve Portuguese decorative arts.

### 16. What was Conceição Amaral’s goal at the summit?

Her presence at the Luxury Summit aimed to change the way people value Portuguese decorative arts and workmanship.
